Introduction

You may know rabbits to be soft, cuddly and loveable, or even as a pet! However, to many people, rabbits are nothing but little nuisances. In some places, these creatures have cause billions of dollars of damage. You may wonder how such a small and harmless creature could cause so much damage.

Well, rabbits are an invasive species. To learn about these creatures, read on!
